About Maggie

With over 7 years of clinical experience in diabetes care and nutrition, I'm passionate about helping people live their best lives despite diabetes. My journey has taken me from hospital settings to cutting-edge medical device companies, always with the goal of improving patient outcomes.

Based in beautiful Colorado, I bring the same determination you'd find in our mountain goats to every client relationship. Whether you're newly diagnosed or looking to optimize your current management, I'm here to guide you every step of the way.
