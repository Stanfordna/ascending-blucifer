About Me

Dedicated to Your Success

I'm Maggie Chamberlain, a Registered Dietitian and Certified Diabetes Care and Education Specialist based in Colorado. With experience ranging from clinical hospital settings to leading medical device companies like Beta Bionics and Tandem Diabetes Care, I bring a unique perspective to diabetes management.
