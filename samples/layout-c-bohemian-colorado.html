About Maggie

A Colorado Heart, A Healing Mission

With the determination of Colorado's mountain goats and over seven years of clinical experience, I've dedicated my career to helping people with diabetes discover what's possible for their health.

From hospital bedside to cutting-edge medical device innovation with Beta Bionics and Tandem Diabetes Care, my journey has always been guided by one mission: empowering individuals to take control of their health with confidence and joy.
